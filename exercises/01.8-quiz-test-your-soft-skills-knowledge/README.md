# 01.8 Quiz: Test Your Soft Skills Knowledge

Put your soft skills to the test with these real-world scenarios! Read each situation carefully and select the best response that demonstrates effective soft skills in a software development environment.

1. Your teammate consistently misses deadlines, causing delays in the project. How should you address this?

   - [ ] Ignore the issue and hope it improves on its own.

   - [ ] Publicly call out the teammate during the next team meeting.

   - [x] Privately discuss the missed deadlines, express your concerns using "I" statements, and offer support to help them improve.

   - [ ] Take over their tasks without informing anyone to keep the project on track.


2. During a code review, a colleague becomes defensive after receiving constructive feedback. What is the best way to respond?

   - [ ] Insist that they must accept the feedback without discussion.

   - [x] Acknowledge their feelings, clarify that the feedback targets the code, not the person, and suggest ways to improve collaboratively.

   - [ ] Avoid giving any further feedback to prevent conflict.

   - [ ] Criticize their attitude to encourage professionalism.


3. In a team meeting, two members have opposing ideas about the project approach. What soft skill should you use to handle this?

   - [ ] Ignore the disagreement and proceed with your own idea.

   - [ ] Take sides with the louder team member.

   - [x] Facilitate respectful discussion, encourage sharing ideas, and help the team find a compromise.

   - [ ] Postpone the decision indefinitely to avoid conflict.


4. You have multiple tasks with tight deadlines. How do you manage your time effectively?

   - [ ] Work on tasks randomly as they come to mind.

   - [ ] Focus only on the easiest tasks to finish quickly.

   - [x] Prioritize tasks based on urgency and impact, create a schedule, and communicate progress with your team.

   - [ ] Delegate all tasks to others without checking their workload.

Good luck! Reflect on your choices and consider how these soft skills apply in your daily work as a developer.
